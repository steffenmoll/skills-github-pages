date: 2026-07-02

#
I'm not usually the kind of person who posts stuff publicly - nor in social media or other channels. I think I've just never really had the need to put out myself and my thoughts. 

This time it's sort of different. I've been wandering around thinking, and it struck me that I've been very fortunate to live in this time of history. 

Everyone in the world (with some exceptions) can post and can express into public debates and disscussions, and **together make atomic contributions forming this massive dynamic organism built on accumulated knowledge**. This is the backbone of our human success. And with the internet the last decades, this has been utterly amplified. 

This time I want to give back to that organism and what I hope could be valuable to others, even though it really requires to go out of the comfort zone - just as the cliché states. 

So here I stand at the starting line - humble of everyone who has plowed the way - taking a step into to sharing both refined and unrefined thoughts, all with the goal of making us slightly more enlightened. You're invited to follow along!
